Students at lambda school need a place where they can escalate their concerns and receive help. This is where we come in.

Dev Desk

Students

  • DevDesk is a platform that allow Lambda students to seek help solving any issue they might encounter.
  • All students have to do is sumbit a help ticket to be assisted
  • Once the student submits a help ticket, our dedicated team are instantly notified at which point a team member will be assigned to the case, and will either resolve the issue or direct it to the right party to solve it.

Admin

  • Sign in to help
  • Admins can be assigned to a specific ticket that fits their strengths
  • Admins are also able to choose a ticket to help students in need
  • After admins complete a task and successfully help a student with their ticket they are then able to mark the ticket as resolved.
